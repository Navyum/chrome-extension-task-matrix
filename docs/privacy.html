Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 22, 2025

Our Principles

TaskMatrix Pro has designed this policy to be consistent with the following principles:

  • Privacy policies should be human readable and easy to find.
  • Data collection, storage, and processing should be simplified as much as possible to enhance security, ensure consistency, and make the practices easy for users to understand.
  • Data practices should meet the reasonable expectations of users.

Information We Collect

TaskMatrix Pro operates on a local-first architecture, which means your data stays on your device. We collect minimal information to provide and improve our service:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Task data: Tasks, notes, and time tracking information you create within the extension
  • Settings and preferences: Your customization choices and extension settings
  • Feedback: Any feedback or support requests you submit to us

Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the extension
  • Error logs: Technical information to help us diagnose and fix issues
  • Browser information: Basic browser and extension version information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the TaskMatrix Pro extension functionality
  • Improve and develop new features for the extension
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our service

Data Storage and Security

Your task data is stored locally on your device using your browser's storage mechanisms. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our service

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access and view your data within the extension
  • Modify: You can edit, update, or delete your task data at any time
  • Export: You can export your data in CSV format
  • Delete: You can uninstall the extension to remove all local data

Data Retention

Your task data is retained locally on your device for as long as you have the extension installed. When you uninstall the extension, all local data is automatically removed. We may retain anonymous usage statistics for analytical purposes.

Children's Privacy

TaskMatrix Pro is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
